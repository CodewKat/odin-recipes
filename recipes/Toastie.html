Grilled Cheese Toastie

Delicious Grilled Cheese recipe

This is a simple quick easy recipe that will be enjoyed by all.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Toast Slices

Steps

  1. Get 2 slices of toast
  2. Add the cheese and onion to one side
  3. Put the other slice of toast on top
  4. Toast the pieces of bread together until golden brown
