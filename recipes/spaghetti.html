Delicious Spagheti Bolognese recipe

This is a simple quick easy recipe that will be enjoyed by all.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Tomato paste
  • Beef Mince
  • Canned Chopped Tomatoes
  • Spaghetti pasta noodles

Steps

  1. Saute the Garlic and Onion
  2. Add the Tomato paste
  3. Add the Beef Mince and Chopped tomatoes
  4. Whilst the spaghetti mix is simmering away. Cook the Pasta according to packet instructions.
